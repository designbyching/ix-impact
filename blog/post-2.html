The Role of AI in Digital Transformation

Published on April 16, 2025 by Mike Ching

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is at the heart of modern digital transformation. From predictive analytics to chatbots, AI is enabling businesses to make smarter decisions and improve efficiency.

For example, we recently helped a client implement an AI-driven customer support system that reduced response times by 60%. By analyzing customer data, the system could predict issues before they arose, improving satisfaction and retention.

AI isn’t just for tech giants—it’s accessible for businesses of all sizes. At IX, we specialize in integrating AI solutions that align with your digital transformation goals, ensuring you stay ahead of the curve.
